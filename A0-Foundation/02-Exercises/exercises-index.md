## Quick Daily Drill

**Conjugate these in your head (30 seconds):**

1. Ich (sein) _______ — I am happy
2. Du (haben) _______ — Do you have a brother?
3. Er (sein) _______ — He is sick
4. Wir (haben) _______ — We have luck
5. Ihr (sein) _______ — Are you good?

**Answers**: bin, hast, ist, haben, seid

---

## How to Practice

1. **Daily**: 10 minutes minimum
2. **Focus**: One topic at a time
3. **Repeat**: Do the same exercise multiple times
4. **Speak**: Read answers aloud
5. **Track**: Monitor your progress

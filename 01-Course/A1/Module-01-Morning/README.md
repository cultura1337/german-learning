# Module 01 — Morning Routine

## Objective
Learn to confidently describe your morning routine in German.

## Contents
- 📖 Lesson
- 💬 Dialogue
- 🧠 Vocabulary
- 📚 Grammar
- ✍️ Exercises
- ❌ Common Mistakes
- 🔁 Review

## Skills
After completing this module you should be able to:
- Greet someone in the morning.
- Describe your morning routine.
- Say what you do before leaving home.
- Ask simple questions about another person's morning.

## Progress Checklist
- [ ] Read the lesson
- [ ] Learn the vocabulary
- [ ] Complete the exercises
- [ ] Speak about your own morning for 3–5 minutes
- [ ] Review after 1 day

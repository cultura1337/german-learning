# EXERCISES — A0 Foundation

## Quick Drills

**Conjugate (30 seconds):**
1. Ich (sein) _______ — I am happy
2. Du (haben) _______ — Do you have...?
3. Er (sein) _______ — He is sick
4. Wir (haben) _______ — We have luck
5. Ihr (sein) _______ — Are you good?

**Answers:** bin, hast, ist, haben, seid

---

## Practice Rules

- **Daily:** 10 min minimum
- **Focus:** One topic
- **Repeat:** Multiple times
- **Speak:** Read aloud!
